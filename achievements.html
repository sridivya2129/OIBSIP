Achievements of NTR

We all know NTR as a great actor, and a legend on silver screen. But he also created political history in his own right, becoming the CM in record time. As a CM, his achievements were countless. He brought the iconic liquor prohibition in the state. More importantly, he is also responsible for creating a unique identity for the Telugu speaking population in the country.
